Career Prospects

Technology Industry

The technology industry is constantly growing as new technology develops, making it a highly sought after industry. Examples of jobs in the technology industry include Software Engineer, Data Analyst and Cyber Risk Analyst. These jobs are crucial for the current 21st century and the ever-changing world.

Software Engineer

Software Engineers mainly use programming to develop softwares, games and more. Software Engineers are vital as they create the basis of any software project. Software Engineers are also in high demand as many businesses require employees to use various applications which are developed by Software Engineers.

Data Analyst

Data Analysts design and maintain databases and use statistical tools to understand data as well as reading trends to support their data. Data Analysts also analyse data to check for the meaning. Data Analysts hold a key role in helping businesses to grow.

Cyber Risk Analyst

Cyber Risk Analysts monitors to see if there is any threat of cyber attacks. They train employees on methods to use to reduce the risk of cyber attacks. They also back-up companies' data on backup servers and upgrades security systems. They are necessary as technology increases in popularity, the number of cybersecurity hazards rises.
