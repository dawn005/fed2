DCITP:
This course is 1 semester long before allowing students to choose 1 of the 3 IT courses to pursue. This course allows students to learn about the fundamental IT modules and IT career guidance for students to better understand their job prospects.

DAAA:
This diploma allows students to pursue AI related careers such as AI Application Developers, Data Analyst, Data Scientist and more. Students wanting to pursue further studies can receive module exemptions or advanced standings with relevant courses offered locally at NUS, NTU, SIT, SUSS, SUTD and SMU.

DISM:
This course allows students to learn how to keep the internet safe from cybercriminals with a curriculum that teaches about offensive attacks, defensive methods and investigative skills. Students can pursue careers such as Associate Security Analyst, Forensic investigator and more.

DIT:
This course allows students to pursue IT related careers. Students can pursue careers such as Project Manager and UI Designer. Students wanting to pursue further studies can receive module exemptions or advanced standings with relevant courses locally.
